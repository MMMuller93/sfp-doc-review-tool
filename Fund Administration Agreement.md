# FUND ADMINISTRATION AGREEMENT

This Fund Administration Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is entered into as of [DATE] (the "**Effective Date**"), by and between:

**[FUND NAME]**, a [Delaware limited partnership / Delaware limited liability company] (the "**Fund**"), and

**[ADMINISTRATOR NAME]**, a [STATE] [limited liability company / corporation] (the "**Administrator**").

---

## 1. Appointment

The Fund hereby appoints the Administrator to serve as the administrative agent for the Fund, and the Administrator accepts such appointment. The Administrator shall provide fund administration services to the Fund, which may include, without limitation:

- Investor onboarding, subscription processing, and investor communications
- Processing and tracking of capital calls and distributions
- Maintenance of books, records, and the investor ledger
- Preparation of investor statements and periodic reports
- Such other administrative services as the parties may agree upon from time to time

The Administrator shall perform the Services with reasonable care and in accordance with the Fund's governing documents as provided by the Fund.

---

## 2. Term and Termination

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until terminated by either party upon not less than thirty (30) days' prior written notice to the other party. Upon termination, the Administrator shall reasonably cooperate with the Fund or any successor administrator to effect an orderly transition of services and records.

---

## 3. Standard of Care; Reliance

The Administrator shall perform its duties hereunder with reasonable care and diligence. The Administrator shall be entitled to rely, and shall be protected in acting upon, any information, records, documents, or instructions provided by or on behalf of the Fund, and shall have no duty to verify the accuracy or completeness thereof. The Administrator shall not be liable for any action taken or omitted in good faith reliance on such information.

---

## 4. Limitation of Liability

The Administrator shall not be liable to the Fund for any losses, damages, or expenses arising from the performance of its duties hereunder, except to the extent such losses, damages, or expenses result from the Administrator's gross negligence, willful misconduct, or fraud. In no event shall the Administrator be liable for any indirect, special, incidental, or consequential damages.

---

## 5. Confidentiality

Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of non-public information received from the other party and shall not disclose such information to any third party, except as required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to such party's professional advisors who are bound by confidentiality obligations.

---

## 6. General Provisions

**Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

**Amendments.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both parties.

**Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior discussions and agreements.

**Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
